## Outcome Notes

- The checkpoint reason registry now freezes all `9` required reason families: `CAPTCHA`, `MFA_REQUIRED`, `STEP_UP_REQUIRED`, `EMAIL_VERIFICATION_REQUIRED`, `DEVICE_APPROVAL_REQUIRED`, `SUSPICIOUS_LOGIN_REVIEW`, `HUMAN_CONFIRMATION_REQUIRED`, `PORTAL_POLICY_BLOCK`, and `UNKNOWN_CHALLENGE_REVIEW_REQUIRED`.

- Redaction posture is now standardized across `5` artifact classes: masked screenshots, DOM hash-only signatures, suppressed traces, redacted safe-copy snapshots, and browser-storage refs only.
- The `portal-checkpoint-atlas` now renders `7` scenario families as a premium pause-and-resume ledger with the required timeline spine, reduced-motion parity, persistent inspector, and stable `data-testid` anchors for reason chips, evidence rows, and resume-precondition rows.
- During verification, the root Playwright flow surfaced a false-positive sanitizer failure caused by the safe explanatory word `cookies` inside a note. The guard was tightened to reject real secret-bearing patterns such as `set-cookie:`, `cookie=`, `authorization:`, `bearer `, and password assignments rather than innocuous prose.
